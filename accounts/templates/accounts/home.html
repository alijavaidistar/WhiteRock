Veterans Benefits
  • Must be admitted and enrolled in a degree program to receive benefits.
  • Request for Enrollment Certification must be submitted each semester.
  • First-time applicants must complete VA form 22-1990 and provide supporting documents.
  • Transfer students must submit VA form 22-1995 or 22-5495 to change institutions.
Term Withdrawal
  • Term withdrawal means dropping all courses and leaving the university for the term.
  • Processing takes 3-5 business days, with the withdrawal date based on submission.
  • Refund eligibility depends on the date the withdrawal request is received.
  • Domestic students can withdraw via MyUH, fax, or in-person, while international students must meet with OISSS.
  • Special exceptions apply for military, medical, or personal emergency withdrawals.
